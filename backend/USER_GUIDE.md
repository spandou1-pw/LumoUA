# USER_GUIDE.md — What You Can Do in Єдина Today

This guide covers what's actually built and working, not the full long-term vision. See `LAUNCH_READINESS.md` if you want the complete, honest picture of what's still in progress.

## Getting started

1. **Sign up** with your email — you'll get a verification code by email. Have a friend's referral code? Enter it during signup; you'll both get a coin bonus once you verify your email.
2. **Onboarding** walks you through what Єдина is in three quick screens.
3. You're in. Your feed starts empty until you follow people — check "People you may know" for suggestions based on mutual connections.

## Your feed

A reverse-chronological feed of posts from people you follow. Like and comment; pull down to refresh. Posts you post yourself can be deleted by you any time.

## Wallet & Coins

You have a coin balance — a closed-loop currency you top up with real payment methods (card via Stripe on web; native App Store/Play Store purchases on mobile, once those are configured for your region). Coins can be:
- **Sent as gifts** to other users (browse the Gift Store — regular, animated, rare, legendary, and limited-time seasonal gifts).
- **Transferred directly** to another user's coin balance (subject to a daily limit that's higher for Premium subscribers).
- **Spent on Premium** cosmetics and subscription tiers.

**Important**: coins are not real money and cannot be withdrawn, cashed out, or converted back to your payment method once purchased. Gifts you receive show a coin value for display purposes only — that value is not added to your own spendable balance.

## Profile customization

Premium subscribers can equip animated badges, frames, banners, themes, and username colors, set a status message/emoji beside their name, and use a video avatar. Everyone (regardless of subscription) can showcase up to a few of their favorite received gifts, earned achievements, and equipped badges on their profile — the exact number of showcase slots is higher for Premium.

## Achievements & Missions

Complete daily and weekly missions (e.g. "send 3 gifts today") for coin rewards. Earned achievements can be pinned to your profile showcase. If you were one of the platform's first users, check your profile for a Founder badge.

## Privacy & your data

- **Blocking/muting**: block someone to stop all interaction; mute to stop seeing their posts without them knowing.
- **Your data**: request a full export of your data (profile, posts, comments, wallet history, gift history) from Settings at any time — see doc GDPR in SECURITY.md for exactly what's included (message content is never included in exports — it's end-to-end encrypted and the server never has access to it).
- **Account deletion**: also available from Settings. Your personal information is scrubbed; your object-storage files (photos, avatar) are permanently deleted. Some content (replies others made to your now-anonymized comments) may remain visible to preserve conversation context for other users, without your personal information attached to it.
- **Sessions**: see and revoke individual logged-in devices from Settings, or log out everywhere at once.

## What's not ready yet

Direct messaging, Stories, Channels, in-app search, voice/video calls, and the Premium/Gift Store storefront screens are not yet available in the mobile or web apps, even though the underlying account/wallet/social features above work today. If you're testing this platform, expect gaps here — they're tracked honestly in `LAUNCH_READINESS.md`, not hidden.

## Getting help

Report any post, comment, or account that violates community guidelines using the report button — reports go to a human moderation queue and are reviewed by a person, never auto-actioned by an algorithm.
